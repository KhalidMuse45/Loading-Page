Sono Alarm

Engineered and programmed an innovative alarm system utilizing ultrasonic sensors to detect the location of obstacles by utilizing different frequencies. It was modeled after a car's backing-up system, demonstrating creativity and adaptability in applying technology to real-world scenarios. I was solely responsible for the design, development, and testing phases of the object, and programming.

VisioGuidance

Designed and implemented with a group of peers to develop a prototype integrating complex algorithms, infrared light sensors, ultrasonic sensors, buzzers, vibration motors, and Arduino Nanos. We conceptualized and executed the objective of creating a navigational system enabling individuals to traverse any environment blindfolded. Ultimately, I demonstrated strong leadership, teamwork, and technical skills throughout the project's development cycle.
